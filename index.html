I worked collaboratively with the team to ensure the design was both functional and user-friendly, allowing for an easy shopping experience.

Technologies Used:

Key Features:

  • Product listings with images, descriptions, and prices.

Outcome: The shop page serves as a key component of the SeaSerenade website, enabling users to browse and purchase products to support ocean conservation. The page is fully functional with a clean and responsive design, improving the overall user experience. The project successfully met its goal of providing an intuitive shopping experience while promoting sustainability.
